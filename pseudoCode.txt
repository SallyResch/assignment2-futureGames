A DUNGEON Game

First user type in its name to make it more part of the story

Then user gets to choose between 3 options:
   1. If user chooses walk further in:
    randomize between 4 different walk-events

    1. cave closes in
    2. cave expands into a larger open space. which overwhealms the user
    3. Air grows colder, breath becomes visible in the dim light.
    4. a faint smell of perfume lingers in the air - completely out of place here.

   2. If user chooses to open the chest infront of them:
    randomize between 4 different chest-events

    1. Finds a beautiful crown of gold, stacked with rubys and other gems
    2. A puff of smoke escapes the chest, makes user feel weaker.
      - Faint
      - 
    3. A goblin jumps out of the chest and runs away with your shoe.
    4. Chest contains a note: "Better luck next time".

   3. If user chooses to yell in the cave:
    randomize between 4 different yell-events

    1. Nothing returns after yelling, empty
    2. The yell returns but whisperd right behind you
    3. A small pebble rolls towards user, as though pushed bu an invisible hand.
    4. An echo replies with "Hello" cheerfully like its genuinley happy to hear your voice
